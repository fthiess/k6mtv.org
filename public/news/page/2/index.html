Save the Date: Antenna Party Jan 18, 2020

December 12, 2019
A new year is almost upon us, and it’s a good time to look over your equipment to be sure you’ll be ready when you’re needed. Come to our annual Antenna Party Saturday, Jan. 18, from 10am to noon at 1000 Villa. We will have antenna and radio analyzers so you can find out how accurate your equipment is. There will be examples of Go-Kits to give you ideas on what you might want to include in your own kit.

2019 Mountain View ARES/RACES Newsletter

December 12, 2019
2019 has been a full year for our ARES members! Thanks to all of you, we have been able to report to the City and County ARES/RACES that Mountain View hams have invested over 4000 hours in city and county training, exercises, and community events. In 2019 our members participated in 4 Mountain View classes, 4 county exercises, 4 city events and 2 city-wide CERT exercises, plus attending county classes. It was a busy and productive year!
